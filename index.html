Frequently Asked Questions

What makes NutriSync products premium?

We source directly from trusted orchards and farms, ensuring top-grade dry fruits with luxury packaging and sustainable practices.

Do you offer gifting options?

Yes! We offer curated gift boxes for festivals, weddings, and corporate events — or you can build your own.

Where do you ship?

We ship across India with delivery tracking. Enter your pincode at checkout to confirm availability.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely. You can choose quantities, mix items, and even add personalized notes for gifting.

What are the delivery options and charges?

We offer standard delivery (3-5 business days, ₹100) and express delivery (1-2 business days, ₹200). Free delivery on orders above ₹2000.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD) for orders up to ₹5000.

Who owns NutriSync?

NutriSync is proudly owned by Shivam Maurya, based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh — blending tech, taste, and trust.
